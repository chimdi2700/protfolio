About Us

Queen Elizabeth Primary School: Nurturing Young Minds, Building Bright Futures

Welcome to Queen Elizabeth Primary School, a vibrant and inclusive learning community dedicated to providing an exceptional educational experience for children aged 4 to 11. Situated in the heart of a bustling suburban neighborhood, our school is proud to carry the name of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II, symbolizing our commitment to excellence, integrity, and service.

Our Legacy

Queen Elizabeth Primary School has been a cornerstone of the community since its establishment in 1967. With a rich history spanning over five decades, our school has earned a reputation for academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement. We honor our heritage while embracing the opportunities and challenges of the 21st century, preparing our students to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Mission

At Queen Elizabeth Primary School, our mission is to inspire a love of learning, ignite curiosity, and empower every child to reach their full potential. We believe in nurturing the whole child – intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically – and providing a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment where they can grow, explore, and flourish.

Academic Excellence

Driven by a passionate team of educators, Queen Elizabeth Primary School offers a dynamic and engaging curriculum that is both challenging and enriching. Our comprehensive programs in literacy, numeracy, science, humanities, and the arts are designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Through personalized instruction, differentiated learning experiences, and innovative teaching methodologies, we strive to meet the diverse needs and interests of every student, helping them excel academically and achieve their highest potential.

Holistic Development

At Queen Elizabeth Primary School, we recognize that education is not just about academic achievement; it's about nurturing well-rounded individuals who are confident, compassionate, and resilient. Our broad range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, drama, art, and community service, provide students with opportunities to explore their passions, develop talents, and build essential life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and empathy. Through a holistic approach to education, we aim to cultivate confident, independent learners who are prepared to thrive in all aspects of their lives.

Inclusive Community

Queen Elizabeth Primary School is proud to celebrate diversity and promote inclusivity in all that we do. We embrace students from diverse backgrounds, cultures, and abilities, fostering a culture of respect, acceptance, and understanding. Our dedicated staff work tirelessly to create a nurturing and inclusive environment where every child feels valued, supported, and empowered to be their authentic selves. Through collaborative partnerships with families, caregivers, and community stakeholders, we strive to create a strong sense of belonging and foster positive relationships that contribute to the overall well-being and success of our students.

Innovation and Creativity

As we prepare our students for the challenges and opportunities of the future, Queen Elizabeth Primary School is committed to fostering a culture of innovation, creativity, and lifelong learning. Our state-of-the-art facilities, technology-rich classrooms, and hands-on learning experiences provide students with the tools and resources they need to explore, experiment, and innovate. Whether it's through coding and robotics, project-based learning, or interdisciplinary inquiry, we encourage students to think critically, solve problems, and unleash their creative potential in ways that inspire and transform the world around them.

Inclusive Community

Queen Elizabeth Primary School believes in the power of collaboration, partnership, and community engagement to enrich the educational experience and foster positive social change. We actively seek opportunities to engage with parents, caregivers, alumni, local businesses, and community organizations, inviting them to participate in school events, volunteer initiatives, and educational programs that enhance student learning and well-being. Join Us
